On this website you can enjoy solving sudoku's.

We use a special designed, from scratch algorithm that makes up a random sudoku for you to solve. At the start of the game you can chose your difficulty. There are 3 options: easy, medium and hard.

Your score is based on the time which you complete the sudoku. This score is saved per difficulty. Whenever you get stuck you can press the hint button and the game will reveil a number. When you filled in all the numbers, you can press the check button en see if your anwser was correct.

Want to start a game? Press the play button and start playing.
